Aston Martin DB5

James Bond's Iconic Car

Overview

The Aston Martin DB5 is an iconic British luxury sports car that became famous as the vehicle driven by James Bond in the 1964 film *Goldfinger*. Known for its timeless design and powerful performance, the DB5 is a true classic.

Specifications:

Engine

  • Engine Type: Inline 6-cylinder
  • Displacement: 4.0 L
  • Power: 282 hp
  • Torque: 280 lb-ft

Performance

  • 0-60 mph: 8.1 seconds
  • Top Speed: 145 mph
  • Transmission: 5-speed manual

Dimensions

  • Length: 4,210 mm
  • Width: 1,725 mm
  • Height: 1,310 mm
  • Wheelbase: 2,440 mm
  • Weight: 1,450 kg

Fuel Economy

  • Fuel Tank Capacity: 80 L
  • City: 15 mpg
  • Highway: 22 mpg

Notable Features

  • Luxury leather upholstery
  • Automated machine guns (as seen in *Goldfinger*)
  • Retractable number plates
  • Rear smoke screen and oil slick delivery system
  • Classic chrome detailing and elegant design

Price

Current market value for a restored Aston Martin DB5 can range from $1 million to $2 million USD depending on condition and history.
