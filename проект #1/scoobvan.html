The Mystery Machine

Scooby-Doo's Iconic van

Overview

he Mystery Machine is a custom van driven by Scooby-Doo and the gang. It is the group's trusty mode of transportation in their adventures to solve spooky mysteries. Known for its colorful exterior, the van is just as iconic as the characters it carries.

Performance

  • Top Speed: 100 mph
  • Transmission: 4-speed manual

Dimensions

  • Width: 2,100 mm
  • Height: 2,300 mm
  • Wheelbase: 3,000 mm
  • Weight: 2,100 kg

Fuel Economy

  • Fuel Tank Capacity: 75 L
  • City: 15 mpg
  • Highway: 20 mpg

Notable Features

  • Vibrant green and blue color scheme with floral graphics
  • Spacious interior for transporting the Mystery Inc. gang
  • Equipped with various gadgets for solving mysteries
  • Comfortable seating for 7 people
  • Classic chrome detailing and retro design

Price

The current market value of a restored Mystery Machine ranges widely depending on the condition and specific customization, but it's generally estimated to range from $30,000 to $50,000 USD.
