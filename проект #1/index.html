About us

At Hollywood Wheels, we're passionate about bringing the magic of film and television to life through the cars that defined iconic moments on screen. Whether you're a die-hard car enthusiast, a movie buff, or someone looking to own a piece of cinematic history, we specialize in offering high-quality, meticulously crafted replicas of the most famous vehicles from your favorite films and TV shows.

From the timeless DeLorean time machine in Back to the Future to the sleek Batmobile from Batman, we curate a collection of cars that capture the essence of the characters and stories that made them legendary. Each vehicle in our inventory is designed to faithfully replicate the original models, using state-of-the-art technology and attention to detail to ensure that every aspect of the car—inside and out—reflects the true spirit of its on-screen counterpart.

Our team of expert craftsmen, designers, and restoration specialists are dedicated to creating authentic, road-worthy versions of these iconic cars, blending classic automotive craftsmanship with modern engineering. Whether you’re looking to add a rare collectible to your garage or simply want to relive the thrill of your favorite films, we’re here to help you make that dream a reality.
